A wonderful reminder from Robin not to try to be palatable to everyone on the web.

It's a well worn path that many of us end up going down at some point, trying to make our personal websites more 'professional' or 'acceptable' to a wider audience. But in doing so we often lose the very thing that makes our personal websites special and unique.

> A personal website sits on the blurry line between a corporate entity and a skate park. Because a personal website has everything in its future; career connections and maybe weirdo web friends out there, potential ad money to be squeezed out of it or analytics to be siphoned.
